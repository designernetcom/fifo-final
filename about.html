ABOUT US

The FD Group Story : Precision in Every Particle

Precision isn’t just a specification at FD Group; it’s our heritage

Founded in 2015, FD Group began with a singular, ambitious mission: to bridge the gap between complex engineering concepts and world-class physical products. What started as a vision to redefine the plastic injection mold industry has evolved into a powerhouse of manufacturing, now operating across three ISO-certified plants and serving a global network of 50+ elite clients across diverse industries.

Our Triad of
Excellence Shaping Ideas

We provide a seamless transition from engineering theory to industrial reality through three specialized divisions:

FIFO Industries | The Architects of Form

This is where the precision begins. Using advanced CNC and VMC technology, our expert toolmakers craft high-precision molds that serve as the indestructible blueprints for your success.

FIFO Polymers | The Engines of Production

Once the mold is perfected, FIFO Polymers brings it to life. Through automated systems and modern injection molding technology, we transform raw concepts into high-quality plastic components at scale.

FIFO Engineering | The Framework of Success

This division specializes in the design and fabrication of heavy-duty jigs and fixtures. By creating essential work-holding and positioning tools, we ensure a stable and efficient manufacturing process.
